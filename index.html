ABOUT US

Dracula's Coffee was founded with the purpose of providing delicious coffee and refreshing drinks to busy people without too much of a hassle.

The idea for Dracula's Coffee sprung to mind after moving back from Seattle.

There we always used to buy coffee from drive throughs, because we were always in a hurry.

After moving back to California, we just couldn't find places to do that anymore and finally decided to take action and open Dracula's Coffee!

We added a little twist to the whole concept and our drinks will be brought to your car by gorgeous vampires.
